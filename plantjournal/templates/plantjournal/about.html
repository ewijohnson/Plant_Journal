About

PlantJournal - a plant catalog system - is a web application which allows for easy tracking of all the needs of your plants! You can add plants and keep track of what light, water, fertilizer, and other things all of your plants need to thrive. It comes with the capability to add notes about your plants, and to keep track of any new growth that your plants display, as well as keeping track of basic information such as what kinds of flowers your plants make and whether or not each plant is toxic or non-toxic. This application is implemented using Django.
